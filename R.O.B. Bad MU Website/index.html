How To Fight R.O.B.'s Bad Matchups

Alexander (Zandomo) Moore

    • Introduction

    This website was created to be an easy to access resource that provide answers as to how to fight R.O.B.'s hardest matchups. To find information on a specific character, click on the characters name and you will be redirected to a text file that explains the necessities on how to fight each character. An example of things that are talked about are how to position yourself in neutral, what moves of theirs to look out for the most, how to edgeguard each of them, and really just everything that you need to know to help you win. Alongside how to actually fight the character, I will also cover which stages are best/worst for us and other miscellaneous info.
